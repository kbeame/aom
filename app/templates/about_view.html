About

Margaret Carter LMP

In private massage Practice since 1996, I bring several decades of massage experience and training to your time with me. Philosophy

I believe each person is a complex system of body/mind relationships which make up the whole. We are not just body, and muscle/bone, but mind, and digestive,skin, other systems. Our emotions, as well as habits, play a part in how we feel, as well as our stress levels, and methods for managing that. The nutrition and hydration we choose also plays a large part. Though my specialty is massage, I do take these influences into account when working together for total body health and wellness for you.
