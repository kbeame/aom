    About

    Serving Standwood-Camano Since 1996

    Serving Standwood-Camano Since 1996

    Margaret Carter LMP

    In private massage Practice since 1996, I bring several decades of massage experience and training to your time with me. I have in the past spent 26 years as a group exercise trainer, which lends a unique understanding of movement and rehabilitative strengthening options to your session. I spent my first few years in practice tutoring at risk students at Seattle Massage School after graduating with a 4.0 GPA. I continue to expand my skills and training, most recently taking Thai Massage classes and to advance my Myofascial Education with John F Barnes PT.

    I have worked in Chiropractors offices and hair salons, as well as my own private locations. Each one has rounded out experience in different areas , like relaxation/spa massage verses injury treatment after work or car accidents. I can literally see restrictions in the body, and often without prior knowledge, can tell exactly what has happened in a car accident by the way the body responds after impact. This is helpful to tell me where I should concentrate our sessions .

    I have been a Stanwood area resident since 1984, raised all 3 grown sons in this community as a single mom, and my massage practice has been serving clients here since 1996. I currently still reside in the same home with my partner Ron and our awesome cat , O’Malley. In my spare time I love to grow food, cook, snowboard, hike our incredible Northwest mountians, and travel as much as I can.

    Philosophy

    I believe each person is a complex system of body/mind relationships which make up the whole. We are not just body, and muscle/bone, but mind, and digestive,skin, other systems. Our emotions, as well as habits, play a part in how we feel, as well as our stress levels, and methods for managing that. The nutrition and hydration we choose also plays a large part. Though my specialty is massage, I do take these influences into account when working together for total body health and wellness for you.

    Whether your goal is

    • stress reduction
    • relaxation
    • managing chronic pain or illness
    • Headache/migraine support
    • recovering from injury or illness

    I can work together with your other providers to make up a complete easy to follow program of care for YOUR personal, best result reflective of your time and your goals. To this end I bring lots of Massage Modalities to provide you with a custom experience.
