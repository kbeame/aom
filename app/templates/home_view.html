    Massage Options

    Relaxation Massage

    Generally more light in pressure, increasing circulation and connectivity in the body; I bring elements of Swedish, Esalen, Lomi Lomi, Thai, Traeger, Manual Lymphatic Drainage to this full body massage. Any or all of these luscious techniques can be combined for a truly increadible "getaway" experience.

    MYOFASCIAL RELEASE and MOBILIZATION:

    This work, pioneered by John F Barnes, is my specialty. It differs from other types of massage because it addresses restrictions and habitual patterns deep within the connective (fascial) system ,which directly affect every other system, including emotional, in the body. This work is done without oils, going just to the barrier, and then allowing the body to “unwind” or release long held restrictions . I love it because it is non-invasive, great for Acute injury when the body won't tolerate traditional work, and only goes with the body’s invitation, so it is powerful but never painful. I often combine with more traditional techniques in a session depending on session goals. Most people will feel better immediately, and I will work with you after treatment to address any soreness or emotions that can arise, as your fascial system often will continue to release several days post treatment.

    Deep Tissue, Injury Treatment

    Specific, structural reorganizing, rebalancing based on client information/complaints and my observations of their particular patterns to injuries. This is a very deep myofascial and muscular work taken just to tolorance working in tandem with the client to release core or new injuries/restrictions, allowing freedom of movement as well as reduced pain and stiffness

    Cranial Sacral

    Working with the deepest layers of fascia, this treatment follows a 10-step protocol to assess and realse myofascial restrictions on the brain cavity and dural tube. Essential for migranes and recurring headaches, it also help immunity and other regulatory systems in the body. Can be combined with Deep Tissue for a truly effective approach to chronic pain.

    Hot Stone

    Ancient cultures have used this practice to produce deep, healing relaxation and warming detoxification of the tissues< I use the stones as an extentions of my own hands, with either a treatment intention, deep clearing and relaxation, or both. Stones can be placed on or above blocked areas, and/or the body's energy centers to encourage release. Extremely grounding in nature, this ancient healing art is a truly unique full body experience./p>

    Hawaiian Lomi Lomi/Thai Table

    "Lomi Lomi" means salmon in Hawaiian, with a deeper meaning of "returning to the source." This deeply moving, prayerful bodywork is profoundly healing, relaxing and energixing at the same time. Yin/Yang energatic interplay, stretches, and full length strokes with movement/rocking are hallmarks of this work. I blend with Thai moves/compression for the ultimate experience.
